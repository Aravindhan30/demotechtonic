Institution & Committees

Institution Profile

About Trust

Vallal P.T. Lee Chengalvaraya Naicker, the illustrious founder of this institution, was born in the year 1829. The trust was started in the year 1874. In the name of P.T Lee Chengalvaraya Naicker conceived by benevolent man mainly for the purpose to imparting free education and free medical services for economically weaker and downtrodden society. As per the order of Hon'ble High Court of Madras, the trust started functioning as a separate entity from the year 1996. Our trust is being administrated by a team of nine eminent trustees drawn from different walks of life and who were holding very high position in the past. Presently, the trust board is headed by Hon'ble Justice P. Kalaiyarasan, former Judge, High Court of Madras as Chairman and other trustees duly appointed by Government of Tamil Nadu.

About College

P.T. Lee Chengalvaraya Naicker College of Engineering & Technology is established in the year of 2001. The College is literally situated in Oovery, Kanchipuram, 8 kms from Kanchipuram and 18 kms from Arakkonam. The College is a private, self-financed Institution, affiliated to Anna University, approved by AICTE. The College is a unit of P.T. Lee Chengalvaraya Naicker Trust, Vepery, Chennai – 600 007. Our College Provides Six Engineering Courses, such as B.E-ECE, MECH, EEE, CSE, B. Tech-IT and AI & DS.

About Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering is the first Program of this college. The Department is fully equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories such as Digital Signal Processing, VLSI, Optical & Microwave and RF Communication and IOT lab. Our department have four Doctorates and three faculty members on pursuing research. Also, our department is equipped with Tanner Licensed software. It aims to enrich the knowledge and skills of the students on the basic concepts and theories that will equip them in their professional work involving analysis, systems implementation, operation, production, and maintenance of the various applications in the field of Electronics and Communications Engineering.
